YouTube Video Downloader

This site is purely for educational purposes. Always like and share the videos to support the creator.

Note:

The initial goal of creating this site was to allow users to freely download YouTube videos by adding 'a' in front of the URL. However, since I don't own the domain www.ayoutube.com, this couldn't be implemented. Instead, users can download videos and audio by substituting the YouTube domain with my free domain from render.com, https://yt-downloader-brbx.onrender.com, which is not very convenient. Unfortunately, without funds to purchase a domain, it's not a viable solution.

Example:

    After Owning a domain

  • https://www.ayoutube.com/watch?v=example to download video.
  • https://www.ayoutube.com/listen?v=example to download audio.

    Right Now

  • https://yt-downloader-brbx.onrender.com/watch?v=example to download video.
  • https://yt-downloader-brbx.onrender.com/listen?v=example to download audio.
